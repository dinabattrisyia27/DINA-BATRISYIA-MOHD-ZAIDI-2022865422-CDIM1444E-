My Skincare Routine

  • Cleanser: Helps remove dirt and oil from the skin.
  • Toner: Balances the skin's pH and tightens pores.
  • Vitamin C Serum: Brightens the skin and reduces dark spots.
  • Moisturizer: Keeps the skin hydrated and soft.
  • Sunscreen: Protects the skin from harmful UV rays.
