Primary School: Sek Keb Abdul Samat (2012-2016, UPSR)

I attended this school from second grade until sixth grade. Memories of my five years are very meaningful in my life, I made good friends and had teachers who were always attentive to me. Many teachers consider me a potential student, and I am consistently ranked first to third in the end-of-year exam. I also always receive "anugerah kecemerlangan" at my school. However, I am only the average student, but I presented a big expectations to both of my parents, they put too high an expectation on me, and I feel really pressured, everything I do at school is simply to make my parents proud of me, but the tougher I try, my achievements are still not noticed by them. As a result, I have become a very quiet student who does not socialize with my peers because I am too focused on my academic achievements. My life has become somewhat boring as a result of my obsession with chasing academic achievements.

Secondary School: SMK (P) Kapar (2017-2021, SPM)

I began a new life in this school, where I encountered many different types of human behavior, and where I met my true friend, Daewi. I have been friends with Daewi since I was 13 years old, and I am still friends with her now that I am 20 years old. Our relationship is very close, and we support each other a lot. I attended a girls' high school and grew up with extremely girls-girls behavior, such as getting along with my friends, studying together, and doing everything with my girls. I started to be gentle with myself, treat myself as no one else does for me. I am no longer too harsh on myself; maybe hard, but not as hard as previously. I try to enjoy my life more, but I still study hard.

University: UiTM Merbok (2022-present, Diploma in Informatic Library)

I entered a new world, yes, adulthood, with various challenges and barriers to overcome, beefing with myself a lot, I began to change myself to become a more mature person and constantly improve myself, as well as often reflex myself. Being a university student is not as easy as it appears; yet, the obstacles of my life in high school were more challenging, but I always remained cool during any terrible day. But the challenge at university is really difficult for me; I face presure and extreme stress, and my mental health is ups and down because I care too much about my academics; I become uneasy because there are too many friends who are too brilliant; It makes me feel like I'm always falling behind, yet I manage to look good so that I don't appear too weak. I have a small circle of friends, but nothing makes me happier than having friends who understand me when I'm not in a good mood, who have helped me a lot when I've messed up with myself, who support me well, and someone that sincerely friends with me even though I have many flaws. I am very grateful to them. I hope we keep taking care of each other and go up to the sky chasing big dreams.
